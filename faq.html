Security & Privacy

4 questions
Yes, fully. AdminCat is built and hosted in Frankfurt, Germany, and complies 100% with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We never sell your data to third parties, and you maintain full ownership of all your documents and records at all times.
AdminCat uses double encryption — your data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and encrypted at rest using AES-256. This means even if someone intercepted your data, it would be completely unreadable without your private keys.
Yes. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA on your account. You can use an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy) or receive codes via SMS. 2FA can be enabled in your account Security Settings.
Yes, you have the right to erasure. You can request complete account deletion from your account settings at any time. All your personal data, documents, and records will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of your request, in line with GDPR requirements.

Billing & Plans

4 questions
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, SEPA Direct Debit for European customers, and bank transfers for annual enterprise plans. We support over 50 currencies so you can pay in your local currency.
Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your billing settings. You'll retain access to your paid plan until the end of your current billing period, after which your account reverts to the free tier.
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason within the first 14 days, contact our support team and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account's Billing section. When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next renewal date.

Features

4 questions
Family Spaces lets you create a shared workspace for your household. Each member gets their own private area while you can also share documents, reminders, and records across the family. You can manage permissions for each member — deciding exactly what they can see and edit.
AdminCat supports all common file types including PDFs, Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, images (JPG, PNG, HEIC), and more. There is no single file size limit on paid plans. The free plan supports files up to 25MB each.
You can set up automated reminders for any document, deadline, or recurring event — like passport renewal, insurance renewal, or contract expiry. AdminCat will send you email notifications (and in-app alerts) at your chosen intervals so nothing slips through the cracks.
Yes. You can export all your documents as a ZIP archive and export your records and data as CSV or PDF at any time. This is available from your account settings under Data Export. We believe your data belongs to you, always.
